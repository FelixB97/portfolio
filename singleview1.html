Splash design

If you wanna try out the application or look at the design first hand:

link to full resolution: Splash design image

As part of one of our first design assignments at KEA we had to create a splash design of an assigned art period. I was assigned Post modernism. Using what little photoshop skills we had acquired at that point, we all came up with our own individual ideas of what our given period splash could look like. Here the important part of the assignment was not only to represent our given art period but also create a visually appealing piece that made sense and obeyed most of the general design principles we where taught.
