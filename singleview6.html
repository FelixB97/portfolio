As part of the exam project this summer we had to create a dashboard site for a made up bar, and a form site for the customers to use in the made up bar. In our case we decided on a space themed bar and spent a lot of time figuring out how our bar site would look, after that we decided on the core functions that our site should provide, and then planned how to structure our work. The result we were proud of and it was a really fun project using most of the knowledge we had accumulated over the last 3 semesters leaving us satisfied with a professional and good looking end product.
