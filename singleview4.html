Form design project

If you wanna try out the application or look at the design first hand:

link to the: Form design

The primary reason for this assignment was to teach us the principles of how to create a good form flow and design. Exploring different CSS pseudo selectors and staying true to the UX guide lines we were taught in class. Here KEA had partnered up with Signifly a digital design agency who had Ezone and esports organisation as their client. Our goal was then to create a sign-up form for their website that felt coherent with their design, and would make more visitors sign up with their service.
